For over four decades, we've been transforming houses into extraordinary homes across Central Florida.

Our Story

Luxury Home Transformations was founded on a simple belief: every homeowner deserves a space that reflects their highest aspirations.

Since 1985, we've been dedicated to crafting exceptional living spaces across Central Florida. What began as a small family operation has grown into the region's most trusted luxury renovation company, yet we've never lost sight of the personal touch that defines our work.

We approach every project as a unique opportunity to create something extraordinary. Our team of designers, craftspeople, and project managers work in concert to deliver transformations that exceed expectations and stand the test of time.

Our Heritage

Built on a Legacy of Excellence

Bespoke Residential Projects

Our bespoke residential projects reflect a deep commitment to excellence. From concept to completion, we collaborate closely with our clients to create homes that feel refined, intentional, and beautifully designed.

40+ Years of International Experience

Backed by more than 40 years of construction and development experience through the Paznat Group in Israel, our team brings a unique blend of craftsmanship, innovation, and full-scale project expertise. As an international company, we manage every stage under one roof — planning, design, construction, customization, and post-delivery support — ensuring a seamless and elevated experience.

Passion for Quality

Our work is guided by a passion for thoughtful building and modern living. With a multidisciplinary team of seasoned professionals, we focus on quality, precision, and trusted long-term relationships with homeowners and investors.

Leadership & Vision

LUXHT is led by founder Ouzi Morad, a third-generation contractor with over 40 years of industry experience, and CEO Ruth Morad. Together, they bring a vision of luxury renovation grounded in professionalism, integrity, and a commitment to exceptional service.
